Reminder of Scratch Rules

Scratches are not permitted. Swimmers who fail to appear for any competition in which they have entered will be disqualified from further competition.
Previous performances will not be nullified. An athlete and team will not be penalized for the inability to compete because of illness.
An individual swimmer or relay team may withdraw from competition without penalty in any given event by declaring a false start. Declared false starts, however, do count as an event.

TL;DR - Do NOT miss an event. If you are in a situation where you must miss one, contact Coach Jones to discuss a declared false start.
